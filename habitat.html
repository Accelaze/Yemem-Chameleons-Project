A Chameleon's Cage

A Chameleon's cage should be filled with live plants and be big enough to mimic a small forest; it should have large plants big enough for the chameleon to hide inside it from the UVB light and us. The cage should have variours accessories like large sticks for the chameleon to climb onto.
