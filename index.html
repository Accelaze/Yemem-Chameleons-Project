There are many different kinds of chameleons, ranging from the Yemen/Veiled Chameleon (the one above) which are one of the best options when taking chameleons as pets for the first time, to the biggest ones: Parson's chameleons. The Yemen/Veiled chameleons are native to Saudi Arabia.

Adult males Yemen/Veiled chameleons can grow up to 17 to 24 inches, including their tail, while the females are a little smaller, growing up to 10 to 14 inches.

Veiled/Yemen Chameleons have distinct and sometimes they are quite dramatic. These chameleons can often be described as cranky and aggressive and only a select few are said to be friendlyj, though they can be tamed to an extent.
